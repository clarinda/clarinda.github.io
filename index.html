Hello! My name is Claire. I live in Cheney and have for far longer than anyone should ever have to live here. My mother and our three cats also reside here with me. Their names are Tom, Tucker, and Chloe.

I am a senior at Eastern Washington University studying Technical Communication with minors in Linguistics and Visual Communication & Design. My intent upon graduation is to enter the field of editing and publication with my sights set on fantasy and science fiction. To this end, I have focused my studies on editing, design, and grammar. I have an intense passion for grammar and can spot a comma splice from fifty paces.
