Resource Guide

Spokane DSHS

client-based project

Context

My group worked closely with the Spokane branch of the Department of Social and Health Services to update and redesign their resource guide. This guide is used to refer clients to local services for each specific need.

I designed these two-sided brochures to accompany the guide as a deliverable for the social workers to give to their clients.

Our resource guide and brochures were chosen over other groups’ versions by the DSHS to be used in their everyday work.

Skills

  • Fact checking and research
  • Development of clear layout and typography
