client-based project

Context

My group worked with Eastern Washington University’s IT Department to make new instructions for projector carts used in some classrooms.

We sought out participants to test the effectiveness of instructions we had compiled and documented the results of the usability test.

Skills

  • Working individually and in a team to complete a multifaceted project
